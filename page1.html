SWORDS

It is a melee weapon that can both chop and stab

It is composed of a long blade, a hilt, and scabbard.

The Hilt is composed of a grip, and a pommel for conterweight.

Facts about the sword:

  • It is the most versatile weapon.
  • Though it can chop and stab, an axe is still better at chopping and the spear is better at stabbing.
  • There are many types of sword, these include longswords, two-handed, two-bladed, and curved.
